Angular Forms
---------------------------------

2. reactive forms
-----------------

- reactive forms in angular are a way to create forms using a reactive style, in which form logic is maintained in the component class.

- they provide more control and flexibility than templae-driven forms.

- use FormGroup to represent form as a whole.

- use FormControl to represent individual form controls.

- FormGroup takes the form state and validations.

- submit the form by accessing form dara and validating with FormGroup.

- don't forget to import ReactiveFormsModule from @angular/forms
